# Codemars Corporation Employee Handbook

Department: General
Audience: All employees
Confidentiality: Internal

Codemars Corporation is a software and AI services company focused on secure internal automation, enterprise analytics, and customer support tooling. This handbook describes workplace expectations that are available to all employees.

## Working Hours

Standard collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local team time, Monday through Friday. Employees may work flexible hours outside that window if their manager approves and team coverage is maintained.

## Time Off

Employees receive 20 paid vacation days per calendar year. Vacation requests should be submitted at least 10 business days before the planned start date. Managers approve requests based on team coverage.

Employees receive 8 wellness days per year. Wellness days can be used for short illness, personal recovery, or mental health needs. Wellness days do not require a medical certificate unless the absence lasts more than three consecutive business days.

## Remote Work

Codemars supports hybrid work. Employees may work remotely up to three days per week unless their role requires office presence. Remote work must be performed from a secure private network.

## Internal Tools

Employees use MarsDesk for support requests, MarsID for account access, and OrbitDocs for company policies. Passwords must never be shared through chat, email, or tickets.

## General Benefits

All full-time employees receive health insurance, a learning allowance of 1,200 USD per year, and access to the Codemars wellness program.
