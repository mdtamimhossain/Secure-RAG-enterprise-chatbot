# Codemars Corporation Employee Handbook

Department: General
Audience: All employees
Confidentiality: Internal

Codemars Corporation is a software and AI services company focused on secure internal automation, enterprise analytics, and customer support tooling. This handbook describes workplace expectations that are available to all employees.

## Working Hours

Standard collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local team time, Monday through Friday. Employees may work flexible hours outside that window if their manager approves and team coverage is maintained.

## Time Off

Employees receive 20 paid vacation days per calendar year. Vacation requests should be submitted at least 10 business days before the planned start date. Managers approve requests based on team coverage.

Employees receive 8 wellness days per year. Wellness days can be used for short illness, personal recovery, or mental health needs. Wellness days do not require a medical certificate unless the absence lasts more than three consecutive business days.

Employees receive 10 paid sick days per calendar year. Sick days may be used for illness, injury, recovery, medical appointments, preventive care, or short-term health needs. Employees should notify their manager before the start of the workday when they need to take sick leave.

If a sickness absence lasts more than 3 consecutive workdays, Codemars may require a medical certificate through PeopleHub. Employees should not upload medical documents to OrbitDocs, shared drives, or chat channels.

Employees may carry over up to 5 unused vacation days into the next year. Carried-over vacation must normally be used by March 31. Unused sick leave and wellness days do not carry over unless local law requires it.

Codemars provides up to 3 paid emergency leave days per year for urgent family, caregiving, household, or personal emergencies. Emergency leave should be reported as soon as practical and recorded in PeopleHub after the employee returns.

Employees receive up to 5 paid bereavement leave days for immediate family bereavement. HR can advise employees when additional time, travel time, or unpaid leave may be available.

Employees receive 2 floating holidays per year for cultural, religious, civic, or personal observances. Floating holidays must be used within the calendar year.

## Remote Work

Codemars supports hybrid work. Employees may work remotely up to three days per week unless their role requires office presence. Remote work must be performed from a secure private network.

## Internal Tools

Employees use MarsDesk for support requests, MarsID for account access, and OrbitDocs for company policies. Passwords must never be shared through chat, email, or tickets.

## General Benefits

All full-time employees receive health insurance, 10 paid sick days, 20 paid vacation days, 8 wellness days, 2 floating holidays, a learning allowance of 1,200 USD per year, and access to the Codemars wellness program.

## Bonus And Recognition

Codemars may provide annual performance bonuses based on company performance, department goals, individual performance, and employment eligibility. A bonus is not guaranteed unless it is written into an employment contract or approved in writing by Finance and HR.

Employees must be actively employed on the bonus payout date to receive a discretionary annual bonus. Employees on approved protected leave remain eligible unless local law or a written plan says otherwise.

Codemars also provides spot recognition awards for exceptional work, customer impact, operational improvements, security improvements, mentoring, or cross-team collaboration. Spot awards are reviewed by managers and People Operations.
