# Codemars Corporation Helpdesk Support Guide

## Purpose

The Codemars helpdesk supports employees with account access, laptop issues,
approved software, VPN connectivity, and internal tools.

## Support Channels

Employees should submit standard support requests through the Codemars Service
Portal. Urgent account lockouts can be reported through the emergency support
channel listed in the portal.

## Service Targets

Password resets are normally handled within four business hours. Laptop hardware
issues are triaged within one business day. New software requests require manager
approval and security review before installation.

## Employee Responsibilities

Employees should not share passwords, recovery codes, or one-time passcodes with
anyone, including helpdesk staff. The helpdesk will never ask for a password.
