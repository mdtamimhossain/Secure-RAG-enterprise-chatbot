# Codemars Corporation Meeting and Communication Norms

## Meeting Principles

Codemars meetings should have a clear owner, agenda, and desired outcome.
Recurring meetings should be reviewed every quarter to confirm they are still
useful.

## Async Communication

Project updates should be written in the relevant team channel or project page
before broad meetings are scheduled. Decisions should be recorded with the date,
decision owner, and next action.

## Response Expectations

Direct messages are useful for quick coordination, but project decisions should
not live only in private chats. Team members should use shared documentation for
decisions that affect delivery, customers, budget, or security.

## Escalation

Blocked work should be escalated to the project owner after one business day if
the blocker affects delivery commitments.
