# Codemars General Employee Knowledge Base 2026

Department: General
Access: All employees
Document owner: Internal Operations

This file contains sample general employee records and policy facts for the Codemars Intranet RAG chatbot.

## General Data Points

1. Company name: The internal company website is called Codemars Intranet.
2. Helpdesk email: Employees contact helpdesk@codemars.local for IT support.
3. Helpdesk hours: Standard helpdesk hours are Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 6 PM local time.
4. Password reset: Employees reset passwords through the Codemars identity portal.
5. MFA requirement: Multi-factor authentication is required for all company systems.
6. Laptop encryption: All Codemars laptops must have disk encryption enabled.
7. Screen lock: Workstations must lock automatically after 10 minutes of inactivity.
8. VPN usage: VPN is required when accessing internal systems from public networks.
9. Public Wi-Fi: Employees should avoid sensitive work on public Wi-Fi without VPN.
10. Phishing report: Suspicious emails should be reported using the phishing report button.
11. Security training: Security awareness training must be completed annually.
12. Data classification: Codemars uses Public, Internal, Confidential, and Restricted labels.
13. Restricted data: Restricted data must not be shared in public chat tools.
14. Customer data: Customer data may only be stored in approved systems.
15. Personal data: Employee personal data is confidential and handled by HR-approved systems.
16. Meeting default: Meetings should include an agenda when scheduled for more than 15 minutes.
17. Meeting length: Default internal meeting length is 25 or 50 minutes.
18. Focus time: Wednesdays from 1 PM to 4 PM are recommended focus hours.
19. Calendar etiquette: Employees should keep calendars updated with working location.
20. Slack response: Non-urgent chat messages may be answered within one business day.
21. Email response: Standard email response expectation is within two business days.
22. Document storage: Team documents should be stored in approved shared drives.
23. File naming: Shared documents should include date, team, and topic in the title.
24. Version control: Code and technical docs should use GitHub where appropriate.
25. Office access: Employees must use their own badge to enter Codemars offices.
26. Visitor policy: Visitors must sign in and be escorted by an employee.
27. Lost badge: Lost badges must be reported to Security and HR immediately.
28. Desk booking: Hybrid employees reserve desks using the workspace booking tool.
29. Conference rooms: Rooms should be released if not used within 10 minutes.
30. Remote work setup: Employees should use a private work area for confidential calls.
31. Equipment request: Equipment requests are submitted through the IT service portal.
32. Laptop refresh: Standard laptop refresh cycle is 3 years.
33. Software request: Software requests require manager approval and security review.
34. Admin access: Local admin access requires IT approval and expires after 24 hours.
35. Incident report: Security incidents must be reported within 1 hour of discovery.
36. Data loss: Lost devices or suspected data leaks must be reported immediately.
37. Acceptable use: Company systems are for business use and limited personal use.
38. AI tool usage: Confidential data must not be entered into unapproved AI tools.
39. Approved AI tools: Internal AI tools are listed in the Codemars AI registry.
40. RAG chatbot: The Codemars chatbot answers questions from approved internal documents.
41. Chatbot limitation: The chatbot may say it cannot find information when documents do not contain an answer.
42. Chatbot sources: Source references appear only when the answer uses company documents.
43. Directory profile: Employee directory profiles show name, team, role, and location.
44. Directory updates: Employees update directory information through the profile portal.
45. Team channels: Each department maintains one official announcement channel.
46. All-hands: Company all-hands meetings occur on the first Thursday of each month.
47. Office announcements: Facilities posts office updates in the Workplace channel.
48. Emergency alert: Emergency alerts are sent by email, SMS, and company chat.
49. Travel safety: Employees traveling for work should register travel in the travel portal.
50. Accessibility: Accessibility requests for tools or office space go through People Operations.
51. Procurement help: General purchase questions start with the procurement request form.
52. Expense help: Reimbursement questions should be sent to Finance Operations.
53. HR help: Employee policy questions should be sent to People Operations.
54. Manager approval: Most operational requests require direct manager approval.
55. Change management: Production system changes must follow the change management process.
56. Incident severity: Incidents are classified as SEV1, SEV2, SEV3, or SEV4.
57. SEV1 definition: SEV1 means critical customer impact or major business outage.
58. Postmortem: SEV1 and SEV2 incidents require a written postmortem.
59. Knowledge base update: Teams should update internal docs after major process changes.
60. Data retention: Internal documents should follow the owner department retention schedule.
61. Brand assets: Official logos and templates are available in the brand portal.
62. External speaking: Public conference talks require manager and communications approval.
63. Social media: Employees should not disclose confidential company information on social media.
64. Customer calls: Customer-facing calls should use approved meeting links.
65. Recording notice: Meeting recordings require notice to participants.
66. Translation support: Translation support is available for major employee communications.
67. Office snacks: Office snack preferences are collected quarterly.
68. Parking: Office parking rules are maintained by Facilities.
69. Mailroom: Package pickup notifications are sent by Facilities.
70. Sustainability: Employees are encouraged to recycle electronics through IT.
71. Internal survey: Operational satisfaction surveys run twice a year.
72. Knowledge owner: Each internal document should list an owner and review date.
73. Policy conflict: If policies conflict, the more restrictive policy applies until clarified.
74. Escalation path: Start with manager, then department operations, then executive sponsor.
75. General support: General intranet support questions go to operations@codemars.local.
