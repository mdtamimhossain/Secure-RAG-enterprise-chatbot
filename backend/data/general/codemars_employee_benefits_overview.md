# Codemars Corporation Employee Benefits Overview

Department: General
Audience: All employees
Confidentiality: Internal

Codemars provides a standard benefits package to eligible full-time employees.

## Health Coverage

Employees can enroll in medical, dental, and vision coverage during onboarding or annual enrollment. Coverage begins on the first day of the month after the employee start date.

## Learning Allowance

Each full-time employee has a 1,200 USD annual learning allowance. Approved uses include technical courses, professional certifications, books, and conference registration.

## Wellness Program

Employees may use wellness days, mental health resources, and a monthly fitness reimbursement of up to 50 USD.

## Equipment

Employees receive a company laptop, charger, headset, and optional external monitor. Equipment remains Codemars property and must be returned during offboarding.
