# Codemars Corporation Remote Work Guidelines

## Scope

These guidelines apply to all Codemars Corporation employees who work remotely,
hybrid, or from a satellite office.

## Availability Expectations

Employees should keep their calendar updated, respond to direct team messages
within the working day, and join required project ceremonies on time.

Core collaboration hours are 10:00 to 15:00 in the employee's local team time
zone unless a manager approves a different schedule.

## Work Environment

Employees are expected to use a private workspace when discussing internal
projects, customer information, financial plans, or employee matters.

Company laptops must be locked when not in use. Public Wi-Fi should be avoided
unless the employee uses the approved Codemars VPN.

## Office Visits

Teams may schedule monthly office collaboration days. Travel for these days
requires manager approval before booking.
