# Codemars Corporation Leave And Bonus FAQ

Department: General
Audience: All employees
Confidentiality: Internal

This FAQ gives employees a simple summary of common Codemars leave, bonus, and time-off questions. HR policy documents remain the source of truth for restricted case handling.

## Sick Leave

Full-time employees receive 10 paid sick days per calendar year. Sick leave can be used for illness, injury, medical appointments, preventive care, recovery after treatment, or short-term health needs.

Employees should tell their manager before the start of the workday when they need sick leave. If the employee cannot contact the manager, they may notify HR through PeopleHub.

A medical certificate may be required when sick leave is longer than 3 consecutive workdays. Employees should upload medical certificates only through PeopleHub.

Unused sick leave does not carry over into the next year and is not paid out when employment ends, unless local law requires a different rule.

## Vacation Leave

Full-time employees receive 20 paid vacation days per calendar year. Vacation requests should be submitted in PeopleHub at least 10 business days before the planned start date.

Managers approve vacation requests based on team coverage, project deadlines, customer support needs, and fairness across the team.

Employees may carry over up to 5 unused vacation days into the next year. Carried-over vacation should normally be used by March 31.

Vacation requests longer than 10 consecutive business days need manager approval. Vacation longer than 20 consecutive business days may need HR approval and a coverage plan.

## Wellness Days

Employees receive 8 wellness days per year. Wellness days can be used for short illness, personal recovery, mental health needs, burnout prevention, or rest.

Wellness days do not normally require medical documentation unless the absence lasts more than 3 consecutive business days.

Wellness days do not carry over into the next year and are not paid out during offboarding.

## Emergency Leave

Codemars provides up to 3 paid emergency leave days per year for urgent family matters, caregiving emergencies, household emergencies, sudden travel disruption, or serious personal disruption.

Employees should notify their manager or HR as soon as practical. Emergency leave can be recorded after the employee returns if the situation prevents advance notice.

## Bereavement Leave

Employees receive up to 5 paid bereavement leave days for the death of an immediate family member. HR may approve additional unpaid time when travel, cultural observance, or estate responsibilities require more time.

## Parental Leave

Eligible employees may receive up to 12 weeks of paid parental leave. Employees should contact HR early so HR can confirm eligibility, record the schedule, and help the manager plan coverage.

## Floating Holidays

Employees receive 2 floating holidays per calendar year. Floating holidays can be used for religious, cultural, civic, or personal observances. Floating holidays do not carry over.

## Bonus Eligibility

Codemars may provide an annual discretionary performance bonus. Bonus decisions consider company performance, department performance, individual performance, role expectations, and employment eligibility.

Employees must be actively employed on the bonus payout date to receive an annual discretionary bonus unless a written plan, employment contract, or local law says otherwise.

Employees on approved protected leave remain eligible for bonus consideration, but payout may be prorated if a written plan allows proration.

## Referral Bonus

Codemars provides a 1,000 USD employee referral bonus when a referred candidate is hired and completes 90 calendar days of employment. The referring employee must be active on the referral payout date.

Referral bonuses are not paid for candidates already in an active recruiting pipeline, agency-submitted candidates, or candidates referred after an interview process has already started.

## Spot Awards

Managers may nominate employees for spot awards for exceptional delivery, customer impact, automation improvements, mentoring, incident response, security improvements, or cross-team collaboration.

Spot awards are discretionary and reviewed by People Operations and Finance before payout.
