# Codemars Corporation HR Leave Policy

Department: Human Resources
Audience: HR, managers, executives
Confidentiality: Restricted

This document is maintained by People Operations and is restricted to HR, managers, executives, and administrators.

## Medical Leave Review

HR reviews medical leave cases when an absence exceeds five consecutive business days. HR may request medical documentation through a secure HR case in PeopleHub.

## Parental Leave

Eligible employees may receive up to 12 weeks of paid parental leave. HR confirms eligibility, records the leave schedule, and coordinates manager coverage planning.

## Extended Leave Approval

Leave longer than 20 consecutive business days requires HR approval and manager acknowledgement. HR must document the business coverage plan before approval.

## Sensitive Records

Medical certificates, accommodation records, and protected leave documents must be stored only in PeopleHub. They must not be uploaded to OrbitDocs or shared in general chat channels.
