# Codemars Corporation HR Onboarding Playbook

## Access

This HR playbook is available to Human Resources, managers, executives, and
admins. It is not part of the general employee knowledge base.

## Preboarding

HR creates the employee profile, confirms the reporting manager, and submits the
equipment request at least five business days before the start date.

## First Week

The first week includes benefits orientation, payroll setup, security training,
team introductions, and role expectations with the hiring manager.

## Required Records

HR must verify identity documents, employment eligibility, signed policy
acknowledgements, and emergency contact completion before the onboarding case is
closed.

## Manager Handoff

HR sends the manager a thirty-day onboarding checklist with training goals,
mentor assignment, and first performance checkpoint.
