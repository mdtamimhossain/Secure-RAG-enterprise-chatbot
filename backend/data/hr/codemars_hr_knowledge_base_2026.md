# Codemars HR Knowledge Base 2026

Department: HR
Access: HR only
Document owner: People Operations

This file contains sample HR records and policy facts for the Codemars Intranet RAG chatbot.

## HR Data Points

1. Annual leave: Full-time employees receive 20 paid vacation days per calendar year after completing probation.
2. Probation leave: Employees in probation may use up to 3 paid leave days with manager approval.
3. Sick leave: Employees receive 10 paid sick days per calendar year.
4. Sick note: A medical note is required when sick leave exceeds 3 consecutive workdays.
5. Carry-over leave: Up to 5 unused vacation days may be carried into the next year until March 31.
6. Leave blackout: December 20 through January 5 requires director approval for more than 2 consecutive leave days.
7. Emergency leave: Employees may request up to 3 paid emergency leave days for urgent family matters.
8. Bereavement leave: Employees receive 5 paid days for immediate family bereavement.
9. Parental leave: Primary caregivers may request up to 16 weeks of parental leave.
10. Secondary caregiver leave: Secondary caregivers may request up to 4 weeks of parental leave.
11. HR contact: Leave exceptions are reviewed by People Operations at peopleops@codemars.local.
12. Payroll calendar: Salaries are paid on the last business day of each month.
13. Timesheet deadline: Hourly employees must submit timesheets by 5 PM every Friday.
14. Payroll correction: Payroll corrections must be submitted within 7 calendar days of pay date.
15. Benefits enrollment: New hires must complete benefits enrollment within 30 days of start date.
16. Health plan: Codemars offers standard, premium, and family health coverage plans.
17. Dental benefit: Dental coverage includes two routine cleanings per year.
18. Vision benefit: Vision coverage includes one eye exam per calendar year.
19. Wellness stipend: Employees receive a 600 annual wellness stipend.
20. Learning stipend: Employees receive a 1,200 annual learning budget for approved training.
21. Certification reimbursement: Job-related certifications are reimbursed after passing proof is submitted.
22. Equipment stipend: Remote employees receive a one-time 750 home office setup allowance.
23. Internet reimbursement: Remote employees may claim up to 50 per month for internet service.
24. Performance review cycle: Performance reviews happen in March and September.
25. Goal setting: Employees must submit quarterly goals by the second Friday of each quarter.
26. Promotion review: Promotion packets are reviewed in April and October.
27. Manager feedback: Managers must provide written feedback before a promotion packet is submitted.
28. Performance improvement plan: PIP duration is usually 30, 60, or 90 days.
29. Internal transfer eligibility: Employees may apply for internal transfer after 9 months in their current role.
30. Referral bonus: Successful employee referrals pay 1,000 after the referred hire completes 90 days.
31. Onboarding day one: New hires receive account setup, handbook review, and manager introduction.
32. Onboarding week one: New hires complete security, HR, and department orientation by Friday.
33. Buddy program: Every new hire is assigned a buddy for the first 30 days.
34. Equipment return: Departing employees must return laptop, badge, and security token by last working day.
35. Exit interview: HR schedules exit interviews during the final week of employment.
36. Resignation notice: Standard notice period is 2 weeks for employees and 4 weeks for managers.
37. Code of conduct: Employees must complete conduct training during onboarding and annually.
38. Anti-harassment training: Mandatory training must be completed by March 31 each year.
39. Workplace complaint: HR acknowledges workplace complaints within 2 business days.
40. Investigation privacy: HR investigation records are confidential and HR restricted.
41. Conflict of interest: Employees must disclose outside work that may conflict with Codemars duties.
42. Remote work eligibility: Employees must have manager approval before switching to fully remote status.
43. Hybrid schedule: Standard hybrid schedule is 3 office days and 2 remote days per week.
44. Flexible hours: Core collaboration hours are 10 AM to 3 PM local time.
45. Attendance expectation: Employees should notify managers before 9 AM if unavailable.
46. Overtime approval: Non-exempt overtime must be approved before work is performed.
47. Holiday calendar: Codemars observes 11 company holidays annually.
48. Floating holiday: Employees receive 2 floating holidays per year.
49. Volunteer day: Employees may use 1 paid volunteer day per year.
50. Employee ID format: Employee IDs use CM followed by a five-digit number.
51. HR case ID format: HR cases use HR-YYYY-NNNN format.
52. Salary band review: HR reviews salary bands annually in July.
53. Compensation changes: Approved compensation changes take effect on the next payroll cycle.
54. Bonus eligibility: Employees must be active on payout date to receive bonus.
55. Relocation support: Relocation assistance requires VP and HR approval.
56. Immigration support: Work authorization requests are handled by HR compliance.
57. Background checks: Background checks must be completed before the employee start date.
58. Contractor conversion: Contractors may be converted after budget approval and role review.
59. Internship length: Standard internship length is 12 weeks.
60. Intern mentorship: Interns receive one assigned mentor and one manager.
61. Workplace accommodation: Accommodation requests are reviewed confidentially by HR.
62. Medical privacy: Medical information is stored separately from personnel files.
63. Personnel file access: Employees may request their personnel file through People Operations.
64. Training completion: Mandatory training reminders are sent 14, 7, and 1 day before due date.
65. Manager training: New managers must complete manager basics within 45 days.
66. Recognition program: Peer recognition awards are reviewed monthly.
67. Employee survey: Engagement survey runs every June and November.
68. Survey privacy: Survey results are reported only in groups of 5 or more employees.
69. Office conduct: Visitors must be registered and escorted in secure office areas.
70. Badge replacement: Lost badge replacement must be reported immediately to HR and IT.
71. Leave approval SLA: Managers should approve or reject leave requests within 3 business days.
72. HR escalation: Urgent HR escalations go to peopleops-escalation@codemars.local.
73. Grievance appeal: Employees may appeal grievance outcomes within 10 business days.
74. Employee relations review: Formal employee relations cases are reviewed weekly by HR leadership.
75. HR document retention: HR policy documents are reviewed every 12 months for accuracy.
