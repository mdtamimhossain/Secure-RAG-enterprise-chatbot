# Codemars Corporation HR Employee Relations Process

## Confidentiality

Employee relations records are restricted HR documents. They may be accessed by
HR, managers involved in the case, executives, and admins.

## Intake

Concerns may be reported to HR through the employee relations form or directly to
an HR partner. HR logs the concern, assigns an owner, and confirms the next step
with the reporting employee.

## Review

HR reviews available documentation, interviews relevant participants, and
documents findings in the employee relations case file.

## Resolution

Possible outcomes include coaching, manager guidance, mediation, policy
reminders, corrective action, or escalation to legal counsel.

## Record Retention

Case notes should be stored only in the approved HR case system.
