# Codemars Corporation HR Performance Process

Department: Human Resources
Audience: HR, managers, executives
Confidentiality: Restricted

The HR performance process describes how Codemars handles reviews, coaching, and employee relations cases.

## Review Cycle

Formal performance reviews occur twice per year in April and October. HR owns calibration, manager training, and documentation quality checks.

## Improvement Plans

Performance improvement plans require HR review before delivery to an employee. Plans must include measurable expectations, support resources, and review dates.

## Employee Relations

Employee relations complaints are triaged by HR within two business days. Retaliation concerns must be escalated to the Head of People and Legal immediately.

## Confidentiality

Performance documentation is restricted. Finance employees and general employees cannot access HR performance files unless explicitly approved for a case.
