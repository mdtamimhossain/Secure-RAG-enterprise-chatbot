# Codemars Corporation Board Metrics Brief

Department: Executive
Audience: Executive leadership
Confidentiality: Restricted

This brief summarizes executive-only operating metrics prepared for board review.

## Growth Metrics

Annual recurring revenue is tracked monthly by the executive team. The current board plan prioritizes net revenue retention, enterprise pipeline quality, and customer activation.

## Operating Metrics

The executive team monitors gross margin, cloud spend ratio, sales cycle length, and support response time. Detailed board numbers are restricted to executives and administrators.

## Confidential Board Discussion

Board discussion notes include fundraising timing, strategic partnership options, and potential market expansion. These notes must not be shared with general, HR, or Finance roles unless approved by the CEO.
