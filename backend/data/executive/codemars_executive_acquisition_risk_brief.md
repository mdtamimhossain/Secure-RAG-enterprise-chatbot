# Codemars Corporation Executive Acquisition Risk Brief

## Access

This brief is restricted to executives and admins.

## Purpose

The acquisition risk brief summarizes confidential leadership considerations for
potential strategic partnerships, acquisitions, and market expansion options.

## Key Risk Areas

Leadership reviews integration cost, customer concentration, data security
posture, retention risk, product overlap, and regulatory exposure before any
formal offer is considered.

## Decision Process

The executive team reviews each opportunity in the monthly strategy council.
Board notification is required before due diligence begins.

## Confidential Handling

Discussion notes must remain in the executive workspace and should not be copied
into general project tools.
