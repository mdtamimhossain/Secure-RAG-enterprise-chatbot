# Codemars Finance Knowledge Base 2026

Department: Finance
Access: Finance only
Document owner: Finance Operations

This file contains sample finance records and policy facts for the Codemars Intranet RAG chatbot.

## Finance Data Points

1. Expense submission deadline: Employees must submit expenses within 30 days of purchase.
2. Receipt rule: Receipts are required for all expenses over 25.
3. Meal limit domestic: Domestic meal reimbursement is capped at 35 per meal.
4. Meal limit international: International meal reimbursement is capped at 50 per meal.
5. Hotel limit: Standard hotel reimbursement is capped at 220 per night unless pre-approved.
6. Flight class: Economy class is required for flights under 6 hours.
7. Business class: Business class requires CFO approval unless flight time exceeds 8 hours.
8. Ride share: Ride share expenses are allowed for business travel and late office departures.
9. Mileage reimbursement: Personal car mileage is reimbursed at the approved annual finance rate.
10. Expense approval SLA: Managers should approve expenses within 5 business days.
11. Finance audit: Expense audits are performed monthly on a sample of submitted claims.
12. Reimbursement cycle: Approved expenses are reimbursed in the next payroll cycle.
13. Corporate card report: Corporate card reports are due by the 5th business day each month.
14. Missing receipt: Missing receipt forms are allowed up to 3 times per calendar year.
15. Client gift limit: Client gifts are capped at 100 unless Legal and Finance approve.
16. Software purchase: New software over 500 requires procurement review.
17. Procurement threshold: Purchases over 2,500 require a purchase order.
18. Competitive bids: Purchases over 10,000 require at least 3 vendor quotes.
19. Sole source: Sole-source vendor requests require written justification.
20. Vendor onboarding: Vendors must submit tax, banking, and security documents before payment.
21. Invoice email: Vendor invoices must be sent to invoices@codemars.local.
22. Invoice processing SLA: Standard invoices are processed within 10 business days.
23. Payment terms: Standard vendor payment terms are Net 30.
24. Urgent payment: Urgent payment requires director approval and finance operations review.
25. Budget owner: Each department has one assigned budget owner.
26. Budget variance: Variance above 8 percent requires a written explanation.
27. Monthly close: Finance closes books by the 7th business day of each month.
28. Forecast update: Department forecasts are refreshed monthly.
29. Annual planning: Annual budget planning begins in September.
30. Headcount budget: New headcount must be approved in the department budget.
31. Contractor budget: Contractor spend is tracked separately from employee headcount.
32. Travel budget: Travel spend is reviewed quarterly by Finance Operations.
33. Marketing budget: Campaign spend must include campaign code in expense metadata.
34. Cloud budget: Cloud infrastructure spend is reviewed weekly by FinOps.
35. Cloud alert: AWS spend alerts trigger at 70, 90, and 100 percent of monthly budget.
36. Capital expense: Hardware over 1,000 may be classified as capital expense.
37. Depreciation: Approved hardware is depreciated according to finance asset policy.
38. Asset tag: Finance requires asset tags for laptops, monitors, and servers.
39. Asset disposal: Asset disposal requires IT and Finance approval.
40. Revenue recognition: Subscription revenue is recognized over the service period.
41. Professional services revenue: Services revenue is recognized after delivery milestone acceptance.
42. Deferred revenue: Annual prepaid contracts are recorded as deferred revenue.
43. Refund approval: Customer refunds over 1,000 require Finance approval.
44. Discount approval: Sales discounts above 20 percent require VP approval.
45. Deal desk: Enterprise deals above 50,000 go through deal desk review.
46. Tax review: New country sales require tax review before contract signature.
47. Customer credit check: Finance reviews credit risk for annual contracts over 100,000.
48. Bad debt review: Overdue receivables above 60 days are reviewed weekly.
49. Collection escalation: Accounts above 90 days overdue are escalated to Finance leadership.
50. Bank change control: Vendor bank changes require callback verification.
51. Payment fraud check: Finance verifies unusual payment instructions before release.
52. Wire approval: Wire transfers require two approvers.
53. Payroll funding: Payroll funding must be approved 3 business days before pay date.
54. Tax filing calendar: Finance maintains federal, state, and local tax deadlines.
55. Audit evidence: Finance audit evidence is stored in the restricted finance repository.
56. Audit response SLA: Audit evidence requests must be answered within 5 business days.
57. Access review: Finance system access is reviewed quarterly.
58. Segregation of duties: No user may both create and approve the same vendor payment.
59. Finance case ID: Finance cases use FIN-YYYY-NNNN format.
60. PO change: Purchase order changes above 15 percent require re-approval.
61. Blanket PO: Blanket purchase orders are reviewed every quarter.
62. Department chargeback: Shared service chargebacks are posted monthly.
63. Cost center format: Cost centers use CM-DEPT-### format.
64. Project code: Project codes must be included for capital projects.
65. Training reimbursement: Finance reimburses approved training after HR confirms eligibility.
66. Recruiting invoice: Recruiting agency invoices require HR and Finance approval.
67. Office lease: Facilities lease payments are reviewed by Finance monthly.
68. Insurance renewal: Insurance renewals are reviewed in August.
69. Currency conversion: Finance uses the monthly accounting exchange rate for reporting.
70. Tax ID protection: Vendor tax IDs are treated as confidential finance data.
71. Financial report access: Monthly financial reports are restricted to Finance and Executive roles.
72. Budget report access: Department budget details are restricted to Finance and relevant executives.
73. Forecast lock: Forecast submissions lock at 6 PM on the final planning day.
74. Finance escalation: Urgent finance requests go to finance-ops@codemars.local.
75. Finance retention: Finance records are retained according to the seven-year retention schedule.
