# Codemars Corporation Finance Procurement Policy

Department: Finance
Audience: Finance, executives
Confidentiality: Restricted

This document defines procurement thresholds and approval routing for Codemars.

## Purchase Thresholds

Purchases below 2,500 USD require manager approval. Purchases between 2,500 USD and 25,000 USD require department head approval and Finance review.

Purchases above 25,000 USD require CFO approval. Multi-year contracts require Legal review before signature.

## Vendor Risk

Vendors that handle customer data, employee data, payment data, or production credentials require security review before purchase approval.

## Restricted Finance Notes

Pricing negotiations, runway forecasts, margin targets, and vendor discount strategies are restricted to Finance and executive leadership.
