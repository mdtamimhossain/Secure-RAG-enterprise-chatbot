# Codemars Corporation Finance Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Access

This finance policy is available to Finance, executives, and admins.

## Reimbursement Rules

Business expenses must be submitted within thirty days of purchase. Each claim
requires a receipt, business purpose, cost center, and manager approval.

## Common Approved Expenses

Approved expenses may include customer travel, conference registration,
approved software subscriptions, client meals within policy limits, and required
office supplies.

## Restricted Expenses

Personal entertainment, unapproved hardware, gift cards, alcohol-heavy events,
and expenses missing receipts are not reimbursed without finance exception
approval.

## Review Flow

Finance reviews expense submissions every Friday and returns incomplete claims to
the employee for correction.
