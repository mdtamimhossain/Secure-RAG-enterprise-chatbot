# Codemars Corporation Q2 Budget Summary

Department: Finance
Audience: Finance, executives
Confidentiality: Restricted

This Q2 budget summary is restricted to Finance, executives, and administrators.

## Revenue Plan

Codemars targets 4.8 million USD in Q2 subscription revenue. Enterprise automation accounts are expected to contribute 62 percent of the quarterly target.

## Expense Plan

The approved Q2 operating expense budget is 3.1 million USD. Engineering headcount, cloud infrastructure, and customer success staffing are the largest cost categories.

## Hiring Controls

New non-engineering hires require Finance approval until the Q2 revenue target reaches 80 percent attainment. Backfills for critical support roles remain pre-approved.

## Cloud Spend

Cloud infrastructure spend must remain below 410,000 USD for Q2. Any monthly forecast above that threshold requires CFO review.
