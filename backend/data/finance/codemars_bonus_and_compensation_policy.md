# Codemars Corporation Bonus And Compensation Policy

Department: Finance
Audience: Finance, HR, executives
Confidentiality: Restricted

This document describes Finance-owned rules for discretionary bonuses, referral bonuses, spot awards, and payout controls. It does not contain individual compensation records.

## Annual Bonus Pool

Codemars may create an annual discretionary bonus pool after Finance closes the fiscal year forecast. The bonus pool depends on company revenue performance, gross margin, cash position, customer retention, and board-approved budget limits.

The annual bonus pool is proposed by Finance, reviewed with HR, and approved by the executive leadership team before individual payouts are confirmed.

## Bonus Eligibility

Employees must be actively employed on the payout date to receive a discretionary annual bonus unless an employment contract, written bonus plan, protected leave rule, or local law requires different treatment.

Employees who resign before the payout date are normally not eligible for a discretionary annual bonus. Employees whose role is eliminated before payout may be reviewed separately by Finance, HR, and Legal.

Employees on approved protected leave remain eligible for bonus consideration. Finance may prorate the payout when the applicable plan allows proration and HR confirms the leave classification.

## Bonus Calculation Factors

Bonus recommendations may consider company performance, department performance, individual performance rating, role level, time in role, target bonus percentage, and documented business impact.

Managers may submit recommendations, but Finance controls budget validation and HR controls eligibility validation. No manager may promise a bonus before Finance and HR approval.

## Payout Timing

Annual discretionary bonuses are normally paid in March after the fiscal year closes and payroll validation is complete. Finance may change the payout date if audit close, payroll processing, or leadership approval is delayed.

Spot awards and referral bonuses are normally processed in the next regular payroll cycle after approval.

## Referral Bonus

The standard employee referral bonus is 1,000 USD after the referred employee completes 90 calendar days of employment. The referring employee must be active on the payout date.

Referral bonuses are not paid when the candidate was already known to Recruiting, submitted by an agency, referred after interviews started, or hired into an internship unless the referral campaign explicitly allows it.

## Spot Awards

Spot awards may be granted for exceptional delivery, customer impact, security improvements, automation savings, incident response, mentoring, or cross-functional collaboration.

Standard spot award amounts are 250 USD, 500 USD, and 1,000 USD. Awards above 1,000 USD require Finance director approval.

## Tax And Payroll Treatment

Bonuses, referral awards, and spot awards are treated as taxable compensation unless local law provides a different treatment. Payroll withholds applicable taxes and statutory deductions.

Finance must not process off-payroll bonus payments unless Legal and Payroll approve the exception in writing.

## Documentation And Controls

Finance keeps bonus approval records in the secure compensation folder. Individual payout details must not be stored in public documents, shared chat channels, or general HR knowledge bases.

Reports used for bonus planning must be access-controlled to Finance, HR compensation partners, and executives. Aggregated budget summaries may be shared with managers when individual compensation details are removed.
