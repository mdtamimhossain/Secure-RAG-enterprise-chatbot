# Codemars Corporation Finance Revenue Recognition Notes

## Access

Revenue recognition notes are restricted to Finance, executives, and admins.

## Recognition Principle

Codemars recognizes subscription revenue over the active service period.
Implementation fees are reviewed by Finance to determine whether they are
recognized at completion or over the service term.

## Contract Review

Finance reviews contracts with custom payment terms, renewal discounts,
multi-year commitments, or non-standard cancellation clauses.

## Close Process

Revenue schedules are reviewed during monthly close. Exceptions are documented in
the finance close checklist with the contract ID, owner, and resolution date.

## Controls

Revenue adjustments above the finance threshold require controller approval.
